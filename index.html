Select the key and scale you would like to practice, and a difficulty level.

In easy mode, you will have to match scale degrees with their corresponding notes.

In medium mode, chord quality cards are added to the mix. You will have to match three cards for every scale degree. Mind that some scales feature certain chord qualities multiple times!

In hard mode, you will have to match four cards per scale degree: scale degrees, notes, chord qualities, and chord functions.
